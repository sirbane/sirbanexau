The three actual problems — ranked by severity

Problem 1 (critical) — The entry signal is nearly random

EMA5/EMA8 cross on M5 is one of the noisiest signals in existence. In a ranging or pullback market — which is exactly what gold is doing right now — it crosses and re-crosses dozens of times per session. At 27% accuracy it is generating more noise than signal. This is the root cause of everything.

Problem 2 (serious) — No ranging market filter

The bot has no way to know when the market is going sideways vs trending. All 11 trades are BUY — it's been buying into a M5 downtrend/pullback repeatedly. A simple ADX filter (don't trade when ADX < 20) would have blocked most of these losses.

Problem 3 (serious) — AI is confirming bad signals

The AI agents are saying CONFIRM to 7 out of 10 losing trades. Either the prompts aren't giving them enough context, or they're being asked to confirm signals that are inherently unconfirmable. The AI is a rubber stamp right now, not a filter.
